Then if people feel it is a goal worth pursuing, it happens. This page explains the best conceivable path if that will is there and pushes aside conflict politics. In that situation, from when the first mission launches, it would take about 50 years to get to the part called The Space Boom.

The path uses known technology, a reasonable pace of development, and a small assumption that artificial intelligence develops at a pace actually rather slower than it has in the last 5 years.
