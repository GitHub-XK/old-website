Realistic Moon Town, Coming Online

What if a group of nations was determined to colonize the Moon, and they based their decisions only on technical and economic merit?

We are building that town - . Ok, not a real town, a virtual moon with a virtual town, full of digital models of all the structures and machinery involved. Designs are absolutely technically feasible and sensible. They are based on an extensive, expansive timeline into the future, grounded in solid science and practical engineering.

We do this so ever more people will have a detailed vision of how our future in space will really be, whenever as a species we decide to truly invest in it. When the day comes that our daily reality shows us how we are part of a vast cosmos - who will we become? We feel that showing how vast this future is, how full of potential, helps us choose to reach for it instead of for the petty things that hold us back.

What We've Done So Far

We've built Cernan's Promise, a town of 25,000 people sited on the rim of Lalande Crater, near the Moon's equator. All of its buildings and most of its machinery exist.

All the basic things needed for a permanent colony have been designed and built for Cernan's Promise. There are installations for producing glass, metals, ceramics, and cast stone. There are three different power stations - a nuclear plant, a solar cell array, and a giant beamed power receiver. There are specially designed flywheel arrays for storing power overnight. The habitats have been built for proper thermal control in the Moon's harsh environment, and to protect from radiation over decades. The massive Earth-Moon transport system needed for both an extensive population and a thriving economy has been designed. There are habitats for intensive farming. There's deep space communications. Radar. Rovers. Cranes. Remote ops. Artificial gravity. 3d printing. It's all there.

All of this has been set in a world on a path. It's supported by a detailed timeline from the first outposts to a day when we roam the solar system at will. So people can understand that world, it is being richly documented. Plans for further colonies already exist.

A Forum for the Future

We intend to take this and make it a community, where people can explore all facets of our future. To this end we must now build a series of services.

  • Turn the models of the town into a place you can explore by avatar - moving around, operating things, talking with others, accessing added media. Put in details and make them beautiful.
  • Provide a platform for making films in Cernan's Promise. All our resources will be made freely available, no restrictions. There will be camera tools to get just the right shots, lighting rigs, rigged characters, a library of animations. Creations can be quick and simple, through an app, or production quality, through use of our libraries.
  • Provide a platform for making games, that can either be played within the virtual world by means of installing mods, or accessed through links within the world. Our resources will be made available freely without restriction. Fees for placing the games within Moonwards will only be charged if there is a fee for playing.
  • Create an app within the world for building structures there. People can use it to build something on reserved plots, which will stand as long as they continue to participate actively. Some options and some plots will require fees, but many standard plots will be free.
  • Cultivate and moderate forums through which users can collaborate and communicate, to add permanent infrastructure to the virtual world, create alternate versions either within it or separately, and develop any of the above projects.
  • Build further space settlements, and all the ships and operations that support them.

To do all these things will take a lot of people and a lot of work. We are building everything open source so we can ally with the quickly growing open-source communities both in 3d modeling via Blender and in game programming through open source game engines - we are using Godot. A platform like this serves those communities well, as long as it is growing. That part will take money. You can fund us through Gumroad, see the Support Development section on this page.

Help us help the world see a bigger future where we can leave our past behind us.
