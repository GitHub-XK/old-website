--- layout: post title: Teatime on the Moon date: '2016-12-27T10:13:00.000-08:00' author: kim holder tags: modified_time: '2016-12-27T10:13:11.803-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2741304674651227490.post-3094790136829284559 blogger_orig_url: http://moonwards1.blogspot.com/2016/12/teatime-on-moon.html --- We choose our path based on what we can see. Crossing a swamp seems only an inconvenience if the ocean is visible beyond it, quickly forgotten once you're on the beach. Space maybe seems more like a labyrinth than a swamp - confusing, empty, dangerous.  What is to be gained by venturing out there is tremendously unclear and looks very far away.
